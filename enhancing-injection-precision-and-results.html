About joint injection

In medication, a joint injection (intra-articular injection) is a procedure utilized in the therapy of inflammatory joint conditions, such as rheumatoid joint inflammation, psoriatic joint inflammation, gout, tendinitis, bursitis, Repetitive strain injury, and occasionally osteo arthritis. A hypodermic needle is injected right into the impacted joint where it delivers a dose of any kind of among numerous anti-inflammatory agents, the most typical of which are corticosteroids. Hyaluronic acid, as a result of its high viscosity, is in some cases made use of to replace bursa liquids. The technique might be utilized to likewise withdraw excess liquid from the joint.
